# Day 01 - Cybersecurity Basics

## What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage.

## Why is Cybersecurity Important?

- Protects sensitive information
- Prevents cyber attacks
- Ensures business continuity
- Maintains privacy and trust

## Core Principles (CIA Triad)

### Confidentiality
Only authorized users can access information.

### Integrity
Data must remain accurate and unchanged.

### Availability
Systems and data must be accessible when needed.
